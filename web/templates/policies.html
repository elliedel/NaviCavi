Privacy Policy

Welcome to NaviCavi! We value your trust and are dedicated to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information while using our platform. Our goal is to ensure transparency and to provide you with a secure and reliable experience.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account with NaviCavi, we collect specific details to set up and manage your profile. These details include a username and password, which are necessary for you to securely access your account. This information is crucial for ensuring only authorized users can access certain features, such as image uploads and analysis.

Uploaded Images

As part of NaviCavi’s core functionality, you may upload panoramic X-ray images for analysis. These images are used exclusively for medical purposes and do not contain any personal identifiers. The X-rays provide the visual data necessary for our AI to detect teeth and identify potential dental caries (cavities). While we handle these images with utmost care, they are not connected to any personal or identifiable information, ensuring your privacy remains intact.

2. Image Handling and Retention

We are committed to respecting the privacy and security of your medical images. Here’s a breakdown of how we process and store these images:

Processing Only

Your uploaded X-ray images are solely used for the intended purpose of medical analysis. Specifically, our AI model processes these images to detect teeth structures and identify any signs of caries. We do not use these images for any other purposes without your explicit consent, and they are never shared with third parties for commercial use.

Anonymization

To ensure your privacy, all images are anonymized before being used in any form of analysis beyond the immediate medical function. This means any potential information that could be used to identify you is removed. Anonymized images are occasionally used to help improve our AI model’s performance, but they are untraceable to any individual user.

Retention Period

We retain anonymized versions of the images for a period of up to three years. This extended period allows us to use a wide variety of data to train and refine our AI model, improving its ability to detect dental issues accurately. Rest assured, these images will remain anonymized throughout this period and will be securely deleted once they are no longer needed.

3. Data Usage for AI Training

By choosing to use NaviCavi, you consent to the use of your anonymized X-ray images for the ongoing training and improvement of our AI system. This is an essential part of our commitment to continuously improving the accuracy of our tooth detection and caries identification capabilities. Using real-world data ensures our AI model becomes better at analyzing various dental structures, leading to more precise diagnoses.

Your anonymized data plays a crucial role in helping us enhance the performance of our technology and provide better results for all users. At the same time, we ensure that no identifiable information is associated with these images.

4. Your Rights

Account Deletion

You have full control over your NaviCavi account. Should you decide to no longer use our platform, you can delete your account at any time. Upon doing so, all associated information, including your username, password, and any other account details, will be permanently removed from our system. Please note that while your account data is deleted, anonymized X-ray images that have been used for AI training may still be retained.

Data Control

While we do not collect personal information from your X-ray images, you have the right to inquire about how your anonymized data is being used. If you wish to receive more information on how your images contribute to the improvement of our AI model, feel free to contact us. We are dedicated to maintaining transparency in how we use and manage data.

Contact Us

If you have any concerns, questions, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please don’t hesitate to reach out to us. We can be contacted at louisphilipdc@gmail.com, and we are happy to assist you with any inquiries you may have.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

As NaviCavi continues to evolve, we may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any modifications to the policy will be posted on this page, and we encourage users to review the Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your information.

We are committed to ensuring that any updates remain in line with our mission to prioritize your privacy. Continued use of NaviCavi following any updates signifies your acceptance of the revised terms.
